Who is Junnamie?

I’m a passionate manga artist with a love for storytelling, character design, and world-building. My art blends traditional Japanese manga influences with my own unique style. Through my drawings, I aim to connect emotions, imagination, and culture.

My Background

Education

I’m from the Philippines and I studied at Metro Dumaguete College (MDC), taking up Bachelor of Science in Information Technology (BSIT). Later, I pursued my passion for the arts and earned a BA in Fine Arts (2018). To further level up my manga craft, I joined a Specialized Manga Workshop in Tokyo (2020)—which inspired me to focus on becoming a full-time manga artist.

Work Experience

I began my career as an Assistant Illustrator from 2019 to 2021, where I gained hands-on experience in manga production and visual storytelling. In 2021, I started working as a Freelance Manga Artist and have been collaborating with clients and creating independent works ever since. This journey has allowed me to refine my skills and build a unique artistic style that reflects my passion for manga.
